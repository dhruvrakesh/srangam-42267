## Book Compilation Architecture

### Multilingual Support
- **Primary Language:** English (academic publication)
- **Parallel Editions:** Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Hindi, Punjabi
- **Cultural Context:** 1,200+ dharmic terms with etymological explanations

### Academic Standards
- **Citation Style:** MLA 9th Edition with Indological modifications
- **Bibliography:** Consolidated master bibliography (3,200+ sources)
- **Index:** Sanskrit term index, geographic index, archaeological site index
- **Appendices:** Correlation matrix tables, chronological timelines, maps

### Digital-Print Integration
- **QR Codes:** Link print pages to interactive digital content
- **Open Access:** Digital version published under CC BY-NC-SA 4.0
- **Data Repository:** Zenodo deposit of correlation matrix and cultural terms database
- **Peer Review:** Double-blind review through Journal of Indian Ocean Studies
