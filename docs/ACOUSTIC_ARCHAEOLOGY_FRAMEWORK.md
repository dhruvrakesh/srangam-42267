# Acoustic Archaeology Framework

## The Kupgal Model

### Components of Performance Landscape
1. **Images:** Petroglyphs (zebu cattle, anthropomorphs, nets)
2. **Acoustics:** 200+ ringing rocks with wear-lane polishes
3. **Movement:** View-sheds aligned with pasture corridors

**Together = Performance Landscape:** Herding communities used acoustic signaling for coordination across millennia.

## Ringing Rock Methodology

### 1. Resonance Mapping
- Frequency analysis (100-800 Hz range typical)
- Percussor material testing (quartz vs. basalt hammers)
- Decay time measurement

### 2. Wear Analysis
- Strike-lane identification (polished grooves)
- Micro-mark studies under magnification
- Replication experiments

### 3. View-Shed Analysis
- GIS modeling of sight lines from acoustic sites
- Correlation with pasture corridors, water sources
- Seasonal visibility patterns
