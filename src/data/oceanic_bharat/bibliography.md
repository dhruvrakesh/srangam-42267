# Oceanic Bharat – Starter MLA Bibliography

**Primary / Translations**
- Casson, Lionel, editor and translator. *The Periplus Maris Erythraei*. Princeton University Press, 1989.
- Jamison, Stephanie W., and Joel P. Brereton, translators. *The Rigveda: The Earliest Religious Poetry of India*. Oxford University Press, 2014.
- Debroy, Bibek, translator. *The Mahabharata*. 10 vols., Penguin, 2010–2014.
- Olivelle, Patrick, translator. *King, Governance, and Law in Ancient India: Kauṭilya’s Arthaśāstra*. Oxford University Press, 2013.
- Legge, James, translator. *A Record of Buddhistic Kingdoms (Faxian)*. Clarendon Press, 1886.

- Sidebotham, Steven E. *Berenike and the Ancient Maritime Spice Route*. University of California Press, 2011.
- Wheeler, R. E. M. “Arikamedu: An Indo-Roman Trading Station on the East Coast of India.” *Ancient India*, vol. 2, 1946, pp. 17–124.
- Ray, Himanshu Prabha. *The Archaeology of Seafaring in Ancient South Asia*. Cambridge University Press, 2003.
- Hall, Kenneth R. *Maritime Trade and State Development in Early Southeast Asia*. University of Hawai‘i Press, 1985.
- Coedès, George. *The Indianized States of Southeast Asia*. University of Hawaii Press, 1968.
- Nilakanta Sastri, K. A. *The Cōḷas*. University of Madras, 1955.
- Apte, B. K. *History of the Maratha Navy and Merchant Shipping*. State Board for Literature and Culture, 1959.
- Suresh, S. *Symbols of Trade: Roman and Pseudo-Roman Objects in South India*. Manohar, 2004.
- Fleet, John Faith. *Corpus Inscriptionum Indicarum*, Vol. 3. Government of India, 1888.
- Salomon, Richard. *Indian Epigraphy: A Guide to the Study of Inscriptions in Sanskrit, Prakrit, and the Other Indo-Aryan Languages*. Oxford University Press, 1998.
