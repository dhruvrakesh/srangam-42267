# Epigraphic Atlas Bibliography

## Primary Inscription Editions

### Kutai Yūpa Pillars (East Kalimantan)

- Coedès, George. "Les inscriptions malaises de Çrīvijaya." *BEFEO* 30 (1930): 29–80.
- Vogel, J. Ph. "The Yūpa Inscriptions of King Mūlavarman." *BKI* 74 (1918): 167–232.

### Võ Cảnh Stele (Champa, Vietnam)

- Coedès, George. "A propos de la chute du royaume de Çrīvijaya." *BEFEO* 30 (1930): 595–604.
- Kern, H. "Bijdrage tot de verklaring van twee inscripties uit de Palembang-residentie." *BKI* 67 (1913): 393–419.

### Kandahar Bilingual Edict (Afghanistan)

- Helms, Svend. "The Bilingual Edict of Aśoka Found at Kandahar." *Afghan Studies* 3–4 (1982): 113–124.
- Salomon, Richard. *Indian Epigraphy: A Guide to the Study of Inscriptions in Sanskrit, Prakrit, and the Other Indo-Aryan Languages*. New York: Oxford University Press, 1998.
